## Motions

Note that all the motions proposed at this EGM have their text available in [our democracy repo](https://github.com/UWCS/democracy/tree/main/motions-26/frep-egm).

## Constitutionally-changing motions results
- Motion to increase presidents and remove shared office from President role (*Proposed by Jakub Kisielewski, Seconded by Edward O’Dwyer, Thirded by Rowan Shields*): **FAILED** (21 *for*, **25 *against***, 1 *abstain*)
- Motion to Change Events Officer to Ball Officer (*Proposed by Lewis Parry, Seconded by Thomas Facos*): **PASSED** (**21 *for***, 14 *against*, 9 *abstain*)
- Motion to add "lead" roles (*Proposed by Rowan Shields, Seconded by Lewis Parry*): **FAILED** (9 *for*, **28 *against***, 7 *abstain*)

The quorum for constitutionally-changing motions was **43** (per Reg 9), all of the above achieved quorum.
