## Welfare Notice
UWCS has 2 Welfare Officers: Raven (Discord: `raven0034`) and Tom (Discord `tomo.li`). We can also be reached via our email inbox at [welfare@uwcs.co.uk](mailto:welfare@uwcs.co.uk).

You can speak to us at any time via Discord, email, and in-person (often in our exec hoodies), for advice, signposting and support. This can be for navigating the uni's support services, or to disclose something not right that happened due to someone's conduct at one of our events, and much more.

*Any discussions you have will stay with the specific Welfare Officer, unless you permit otherwise.*

We hope you enjoy Term 2 - but it's okay if it doesn't quite go to plan, we're here to help!

  - We collected responses from various wonderful exec on Event Communication, EGM/Elections, Welfare, Gender Inclusivity, Socials, Gaming, Academic, Sports & even Tech!
  - This is available at: [https://uwcs.co.uk/news/2026-01-12-term-1-feedback-responses/](https://uwcs.co.uk/news/2026-01-12-term-1-feedback-responses/)
