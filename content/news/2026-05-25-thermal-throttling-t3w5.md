# [Outreach / Ethnic Minority / Postgrad Elections](http://events.uwcs.co.uk/2025/3/5/outreach+%252F+ethnic+minority+%252F+postgrad+elections/)
#### 🕔 Wed 27th May 5pm - 7pm  📍 [PLT](https://link.mazemap.com/tC3XZ8lG)
#### *New and shiny exec roles! Come along for the chance to make UWCS history!*
We're holding elections for the following roles on Wednesday 27th of May, 5pm.

Membership is required by Wednesday 13th of May, 5pm in order to vote.

More information is available in the #elections-info channel in our Discord server.



#### Roles



- **Ethnic Minority Representative** - Facilitate engagement & create a more welcoming environment in the society for students of colour with an interest in Computer Science. Host varied events to support these goals.

- **Postgraduate Representative** - Facilitate postgraduate engagement with UWCS events, and create a welcoming environment for PGR/PGT members. Hold varied events to help create this community.

- **Outreach Officer** - Coordinate with the rest of exec, building and maintaining relationships with external organisations and stakeholders - e.g. sponsors, external speakers, Uni departments and other societies. Assist the treasurer with negotiating sponsorships, and academics with organising external speakers.



Ethnic Minority Rep is a protected role to those self-identifying as members of an Ethnic Minority.

Postgraduate Rep is a protected role to only PGT/PGR students. This role will be elected again in term 1.



To stand for any of these roles, you need to email the DRO with your name, student ID and a short manifesto (manifesto not strictly required).



#### Motions



Constitutionally changing motions must be received by the DRO 7 days before the election.

If they are received after **Wednesday 20th May, 5pm** they will not be voted on.

Non-binding motions can be raised at any point, including on the night.



#### Deputy Returning Officer



A Deputy Returning Officer is an impartial person who is in charge of the election and vote counting.

In this case, your Deputy Returning Officer is **Thomas Sweet** - contactable via Discord (`tomo.li`) or via email at [`returning-officer@uwcs.co.uk`](mailto:returning-officer@uwcs.co.uk).

Should you feel the DRO cannot assist with your query, get in contact with Returning Officer (officially the SU/Student Voice) via email at [`studentvoice@warwicksu.com`](mailto:studentvoice@warwicksu.com).
