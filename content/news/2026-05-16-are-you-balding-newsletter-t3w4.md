Did you know that...

> Extreme finals stress, sleep-deprived coding marathons, and poor nutrition can trigger temporary hair shedding (telogen effluvium) in a student demographic (us) already genetically prone to male pattern baldness (also because of the male-to-female ratio).

I refuse to be bald! However... this is probably true until summer exam kicks in and I see strands of hair everywhere... maybe this is why the plastic surgery side of medicine is the most profitable...........

Again... here is another fact...

> In the 18-to-25 college age bracket, roughly 20% of young men experience early male pattern baldness, while fewer than 12% of young women face visible hair thinning.

This means that I'm less likely to go bald.... Hopefully, I don't. Otherwise... I might need to go to Turkey like all the famous, rich footballers.

Anyways, welcome back everyone to greatness (my writing). I hope exam season hasn't crushed you into oblivion yet and that your eyes remain clear and not bloodshot red from monster overdose! Trust me. It's a horrifying sight. Please never become overdependent on this.
