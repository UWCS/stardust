Responsible for:
- Being a first point of contact to work confidentially with members to prioritise their wellbeing and safety
- Signposting to external services such as Wellbeing Services and Report & Support
- Informing the President, event organisers, or the university/SU where issues require greater intervention
- Engaging with Active Bystander and Report & Support training
- Liasing with the Social Secretaries to ensure sober exec are present at non-sober events
- Being at least one of the sober exec
